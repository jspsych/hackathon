Spring Hackathon 2025

Boost Your Access To Open-Science

Event Details

When: May 12-15, 2025

Where: Vassar College's Heartwood Inn, Poughkeepsie, NY

Overview

We're excited to announce our first-ever hackathon dedicated to extending the jsPsych ecosystem and advancing open-source scientific software!

Event Schedule

May 12: Participant Arrival

May 13-14: Brainstorming and Collaborative Sessions

May 15: Presentations, Closing, & Departure

What We Offer

Full Coverage: Lodging, meals, and travel expenses

Developer Guidance: Support from the team that built jsPsych

Collaborative Learning: Working to build your own plugins with a small team!

Who Should Attend

  • Experienced jsPsych users
  • Researchers interested in a deeper, hands-on relationship with open-science
  • Invested contributors to open-source

Learning Objectives

  • Develop novel jsPsych plugins and extensions
  • Learn open-source contribution strategies
  • Receive direct feedback on best-practices, from jsPsych developers and like-minded developers

How to Participate

Interested but have questions? Email us at community@jspsych.org
