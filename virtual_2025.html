Virtual Hackathon 2025

Overview

We're excited to announce our second hackathon dedicated to extending the jsPsych ecosystem! This two-week event will provide hands on training in contributing to open source software and building custom plugins for jsPsych. Participants will work in small teams to develop new plugins and extensions, with guidance from the jsPsych development team.

Event Details

Start Date: September 15th, 2025

End Date: September 28th, 2025

Where: Hosted virtually on Discord and Zoom

Target Audience

This event is aimed at researchers who have experience with using jsPsych for building experiments. We won't be teaching the basics of how to use jsPsych at this event. Instead, we will be focusing on how to develop and extend jsPsych itself. If you're interested in learning how to build your own plugins, or you want to learn how to contribute your work to the jsPsych ecosystem, this event is for you!

This is also an especially accessible opportunity for anyone who would otherwise be unable to travel for an in-person event.

What We Offer

Developer Guidance: Workshops and live support from the team that built jsPsych.

Collaborative Learning: Working to build your own plugins or modifications with a small team, dedicated project channels, and peer feedback.

How to Participate

  1. Sign up through our interest form: Let us know who you are, your home institution, and what kind of project you would like to contribute to jsPsych.
  2. Accept the invite to our Discord server: Our team will follow up with this invitation after you submit your form. We'll be hosting all collaboration, workshops, and feedback on our server.
  3. Review our developer documentation: We have step-by-step guides for how to painlessly set up your development space.
  4. Choose a project to join and meet your teammates: Use our server's bot commands to assign yourself a project, based on ideas sourced from participants and our community. Once you get to know who else has assigned themselves the same project, you can start building!
