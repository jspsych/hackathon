Virtual Hackathon 2025

Coming Soon!

Overview

We're excited to announce our second hackathon dedicated to extending the jsPsych ecosystem! This three day event will provide hands on training in contributing to open source software and building custom plugins for jsPsych. Participants will work in small teams to develop new plugins and extensions, with guidance from the jsPsych development team.

Event Details

When: May 12-15, 2025

Where: Vassar College's Heartwood Inn & Institute for the Liberal Arts, Poughkeepsie, NY

Target Audience

This event is aimed at researchers who have experience with using jsPsych for building experiments. We won't be teaching the basics of how to use jsPsych at this event. Instead, we will be focusing on how to develop and extend jsPsych itself. If you're interested in learning how to build your own plugins or you want to learn how to contribute your work to the jsPsych ecosystem this event is for you!

What We Offer

Synchronous workshop sessions to learn jsPsych, with live QnA. These will be recorded and posted for asynchronous participants. Discussion forums, question boards for those that cannot attend synchronous sessions. On-call support throughout the day (will this be a zoom with someone always on shift? or a calendly link for people to schedule ahead of time?)
